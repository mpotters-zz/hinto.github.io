HINTO is a New York based design company established in 2012.
HINTO offers accessories and jewelry unisex designs, thinking of timeless quality, elegance and comfort. From the original idea to its finish, we challenge the limits of contemporary design. It is our passion to revive classical aesthetics into contemporary forms.
We achieve this by incorporating the latest technologies in our crafting process.
We want to share our HINTO vision with you and hope our accessories and jewelry effortlessly become part of your life.

2012年にHINTOはニューヨークを拠点に設立されたデザイン会社です。 
お客様に渡るまで、最新の技術と職人の匠な技術により、デザインの限界に挑戦し、最高品質の素材から造られる流行にとらわれないユニセックスな形、そして身体との一体感を覚えるジュエリーとアクセサリーを提案します。
